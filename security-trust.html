Security & Trust

YourOS operates in Google’s world-class security—and can connect to any data store you trust.

Built on Google Cloud

We build inside Google AppSheets. That means your prototype inherits the same protections that guard Gmail and Google Cloud:

  • SOC 2 Type II audited data centers
  • ISO 27001, 27017, and 27018 certifications
  • FedRAMP Moderate and StateRAMP authorizations
  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Granular OAuth scopes and per-file sharing controls
  • 24×7 monitored perimeter & automated DDoS mitigation

Google’s stack does the heavy lifting from day one.

Your Data, Your Rules

Need data from something other than Google? Fine by us. YourOS can read and write to wherever you keep and want your data:

  • Microsoft, Dropbox, Smartsheet, Salesforce
  • Airtable, Apigee, Box, Cloud Database, OData
  • On-prem databases reachable through a secure gateway or VPN
  • Flat-file exports to your SFTP or cloud storage bucket

We’ll meet your compliance team where they live—whether that’s HIPAA, PCI-DSS, or your own internal policy library.

Data Flow Google Highway

Think of Google as the highway and your data as the car driving on it—you’re in the driver’s seat. While we build on Google’s secure infrastructure, you’re free to use your log ins with Google, Microsoft, Apple, Dropbox, Smartsheet, Box, or Salesforce. It’s your data, your choice.
