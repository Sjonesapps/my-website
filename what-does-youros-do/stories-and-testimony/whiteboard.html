Before: High Sales, Zero Visibility

This was a successful business. Their salespeople could close. Their service teams were experienced. But the moment a customer said yes, the operational chaos began.

  • Inventory tracked on whiteboards at each location — with no way to see what other locations had
  • Appointments clashed because location A didn't know what location B had scheduled
  • Units disappeared between locations during transfers — no one could prove where they were
  • Customers waited, got confused, or gave up because no one had a clear answer
  • Teams blamed each other with no shared record to settle the dispute
  • Leadership couldn't see the full picture — just whatever they could piece together from phone calls

The biggest problem wasn't any one location. It was that 25+ locations were operating as 25+ separate businesses — with no shared view of the assets they all depended on.

The Real Problem: Lifecycle Blindness Across Locations

RV dealerships don't just sell units — they manage active, evolving assets. Every unit has a journey: it generates interest, gets serviced, gets prepped, gets sold, gets delivered. That journey doesn't stay at one location. It crosses teams, facilities, and cities.

When that lifecycle lives on a whiteboard, it disappears the moment someone walks out of the room. When it crosses locations, it disappears entirely.

The fix wasn't a better whiteboard. It was giving every unit a live digital record that followed it through the entire journey — visible to every team, at every location, in real time.

The Build: Four Phases, One Unified System

Phase 1 — Replace the Whiteboards

Familiar layout, fully digitized. Every unit, every status, every location — visible in one place. No more erase marks, no more "I thought you updated that."

Phase 2 — Integrate the Data

We connected the sources that mattered — inventory systems, service records, customer data. Where integrations didn't exist, we built the bridges.

Phase 3 — Sync the Teams

Sales, service, and delivery saw the same record, updated in real time. Across every location. The finger-pointing stopped because there was now a record everyone could see.

Phase 4 — Automate the Ops

Status changes triggered alerts. Bottlenecks flagged themselves. Leadership saw what was moving and what was stalled — without having to make a single phone call.

After: One Source of Truth Across 25+ Locations

  • Every unit tracked from the moment a customer shows interest to the moment it's delivered
  • Every location sees the same live data — no more "what do you have available?" phone calls
  • Appointments no longer clash because conflicts surface before they become promises
  • Transfers tracked in real time — no more "I thought someone took it" conversations
  • New hires onboard faster because the process is in the system, not in one person's head
  • Leadership gets live operational dashboards — not end-of-day reports reconstructed from memory

When new needs showed up, YourOS evolved faster than their internal tech team could deliver. Today, it runs across all departments, and data is always there — always live.
