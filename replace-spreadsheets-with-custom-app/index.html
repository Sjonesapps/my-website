Signs your spreadsheet has become a liability, not a tool

Spreadsheets are flexible — until they become the unofficial operating system of your business. Then they become a single point of failure nobody wants to admit is there.

  • Version confusion — three people, three files, nobody knows which one is real
  • Formulas nobody dares touch because something might break in ways nobody understands
  • Manual reporting every week from data scattered across 12 tabs nobody labeled properly
  • People emailing copies of the same file back and forth, editing their own version in isolation
  • One person who understands the whole system — and zero plan for when they leave or are out sick
  • No real permissions, no process control, no audit trail — just trust and fingers crossed
