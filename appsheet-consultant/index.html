When AppSheet is the right fit

AppSheet is Google's no-code app platform — and when it's set up correctly, it replaces the spreadsheets, email threads, and manual handoffs your team is currently surviving on.

  • Your team already uses Google Workspace (Sheets, Drive, Gmail, Calendar)
  • Your current process lives in spreadsheets that are getting too big or fragile
  • You have field teams who need mobile access to job data or forms
  • You need approval workflows without paying for enterprise software
  • You want a real app fast — without a 6-month development project

Custom Internal Apps

Dashboards & Reports

Why use a consultant instead of building it yourself

AppSheet is no-code — but the decisions underneath it matter. A poorly structured data model creates the same problems as a bad spreadsheet, just harder to fix.

  • Data structure determines everything — wrong tables mean the app won't scale
  • Workflow logic needs clean design — messy automations create more confusion than they solve
  • Permissions and security need planning from day one — not patched in later
  • The app has to match the real process — not an idealized version of it
  • We've built dozens of these — we know what breaks and what works
